iOS Android

Before installing and setting up the eSIM, please make sure that:

  • your device is eSIM compatible and network unlocked.
  • you have a stable connection, preferably WiFi so you can download the eSIM properly to your device.
  • please note the installation process cannot be interrupted.

Method 1: By Direct Installation

    1. Log into your SUNDAY account and go to My eSIM.
    2. Choose the eSIM you want to install.
    3. Tap Manage eSIM and select the tab Direct.
    4. Tap Install Now.
    1. Follow on-screen instructions. It may take around 2-3 minutes to finish, DO NOT close SUNDAY eSIM App during the process.
    2. When you see "Your Webbing eSIM is now active", tap Done to finish the installation.
    1. If prompted, under Cellular or Mobile Plan Label, tap Secondary and select or create a preferred label for your new SUNDAY eSIM.
    1. For Default Line, choose Primary for calls and texts and tap Continue.
    2. For iMessage & FaceTime, choose Primary and tap Continue.
    1. For Cellular or Mobile Data,
      a.   choose your new SUNDAY eSIM if you’re already at your destination. Make sure the option Allow Cellular Data Switching is DISABLED.
      b.   choose Primary if you’re still in your own country.
    2. You will then go back to SUNDAY App and get a message on the successful installation.

Method 2: By QR Code

    1. Log into your SUNDAY account and go to My eSIM.
    2. Choose the eSIM you want to install.
    3. Tap Manage eSIM and select the tab QR Code.
    4. Tap Save QR Code to download the QR code image to your device. Alternatively, you could share it to another device or print it out for scanning.
    1. On your iOS device, go to Settings > Cellular or Mobile Service > Add eSIM.
    2. Select Use QR Code and scan the code. If your device is running iOS 17 or later, tap Open Photos to import the QR code image you just saved from camera roll.
    1. Follow on-screen instructions. It may take around 2-3 minutes to finish.
    2. When you see "Your Webbing eSIM is now active", tap Done to finish the installation.
    1. If prompted, under Cellular or Mobile Plan Label, tap Secondary and select or create a preferred label for your new SUNDAY eSIM.
    1. For Default Line, choose Primary for calls and texts and tap Continue.
    2. For iMessage & FaceTime, choose Primary and tap Continue.
    1. For Cellular or Mobile Data,
      a.   choose your new SUNDAY eSIM if you’re already at your destination. Make sure the option Allow Cellular Data Switching is DISABLED.
      b.   choose Primary if you’re still in your own country.

Method 3: By Manual Input

    1. Log into your SUNDAY account and go to My eSIM. 
    2. Choose the eSIM you want to install.
    3. Tap Manage eSIM and select the tab Manual.
    4. On your iOS device, go to Settings > Cellular or Mobile Service > Add eSIM.
    1. Select Use QR Code > Enter Details Manually.
    1. Go back to your SUNDAY App, copy and paste the SM-DP + Address and Activation Code to corresponding fields. Tap Next.
    1. Follow on-screen instructions. It may take around 2-3 minutes to finish.
    2. When you see "Your Webbing eSIM is now active", tap Done to finish the installation.
    1. If prompted, under Cellular or Mobile Plan Label, tap Secondary and select or create a preferred label for your new SUNDAY eSIM.
    1. For Default Line, choose Primary for calls and texts and tap Continue.
    2. For iMessage & FaceTime, choose Primary and tap Continue.
    1. For Cellular or Mobile Data,
      a.   choose your new SUNDAY eSIM if you’re already at your destination. Make sure the option Allow Cellular Data Switching is DISABLED.
      b.   choose Primary if you’re still in your own country.

Before installing and setting up the eSIM, please make sure that:

  • your device is eSIM compatible.
  • you have already enabled the eSIM function of your device. 
  • you have a stable connection, preferably WiFi so you can download the eSIM properly to your device.
  • please note the installation process cannot be interrupted.

Method 1: By QR code

    1. Log into your SUNDAY account and go to My eSIM.
    2. Choose the eSIM you want to install.
    3. Tap Manage eSIM and select the tab QR Code.
    4. Tap Save QR Code to download the QR code image to your device. Alternatively, you could share it to another device or print it out for scanning.
    5. On your Android device, go to Settings > Connections, Network & Internet or Mobile Network.
    6. Select SIM Manager, Mobile Network or SIM Cards. Switch on eSIM when option is available.
    1. Tap Add eSIM card, Add Mobile Plan or Add Carrier and select the option to scan QR code.
    2. Scan the code or tap Gallery or Photo icon to import the QR code image you just saved from SUNDAY App.
    1. Follow the on-screen instructions to start the installation. It may take around 2-3 minutes to process. Once it finishes, you will be able to see the new SUNDAY (Webbing) eSIM profile on the list.

Method 2: By Manual Input

    1. Log into your SUNDAY account and go to My eSIM.
    2. Choose the eSIM you want to install.
    3. Tap Manage eSIM and select the tab Manual.
    4. On your Android device, go to Settings > Connections, Network & Internet or Mobile Network.
    1. Select SIM Manager, Mobile Network or SIM Cards. Switch on eSIM when option is available.
    2. Tap Add eSIM card, Add Mobile Plan or Add Carrier and select the option to scan QR code.
    1. Tap Enter activation code manually.
    1. Go back to your SUNDAY App, copy and paste the SM-DP + Address & Activation Code to the text field. Then tap Add.
    1. Follow the on-screen instructions to start the installation. It may take around 2-3 minutes to process. Once it finishes, you will be able to see the new SUNDAY (Webbing) eSIM profile on the list.
