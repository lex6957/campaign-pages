What is SUNDAY Reward Programme?

The SUNDAY Reward Programme is our way of showing appreciation to each and every one of you. The accumulated reward points - we call them S.miles here - can be used to offset your future purchases. Enjoy great deals that make your next adventures even more rewarding.

What are S.miles?

S.miles are the rewards in our reward programme. For every US Dollar you spend, you will earn 10 S.miles.


You can also earn S.miles by introducing SUNDAY to your friends. Each successful referral will bring you 200 S.miles. 


Earned S.miles are valid for 365 days from the date they are credited. If you make another purchase, all remaining S.miles in your account will automatically extend for another 365 days.
