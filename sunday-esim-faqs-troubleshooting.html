Troubleshooting

I scan my QR Code but the installation does not start. What should I do?

You can install the eSIM by entering the eSIM details manually. On your SUNDAY App,

1) Go to My eSIM section.

2) Choose the eSIM you want to install, then tap Install/View eSIM.

3) Under Manual, follow the instruction to complete the installation.


Or you could check below See Also for detail guidelines.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact our support team via Contact Us of your SUNDAY App.

If my device is network or carrier-locked, what can I do to use eSIM?

If your device was purchased from a mobile network provider/carrier at a special price, it may be carrier-locked and not able to support other eSIM. Please contact your mobile network provider to unlock your device or adjust its settings.

I did not receive the email or any QR code after purchase. What should I do?

Don't worry, the QR code of your SUNDAY eSIM is not sent via email. You could easily find it under My eSIM > Install/View eSIM > QR Code.


Note: If your data plan was added to an existing eSIM which you have already installed in your device, you would not need the QR Code to activate the plan. QR Code is for eSIM installation only.

What should I set 'Roaming' to for using SUNDAY eSIM?

When you turn on your SUNDAY eSIM, you should set eSIM data roaming ON for keeping smooth network connection and staying away from unexpected charges.

Do I need to set up an APN (Access Point Name) when using SUNDAY eSIM? And how?

After installing your eSIM, usually your device would automatically configure the APN (Access Point Name) settings. If not, please follow the steps for APN setup.


For iOS users

1. Open Settings on your device.

2. Go to Cellular or Mobile Service.

3. Under your SUNDAY eSIM/mobile plan, tap Mobile Data Network.

4. If your device has auto-detected the APN, 'wbdata' should appear as the APN name. If not, please enter 'wbdata' (one word, in small letters, no space) as APN under Mobile Data.

5. Tap Reset Settings and restart your device.


For Android users

1. Open Settings on your device.

2. Go to Connections/Network & Internet, and then choose SIM Manager, Mobile Network or SIM Cards.

3. Select your SUNDAY eSIM and scroll down to tap Access Point Names.

4. If your device has auto-detected the APN, 'wbdata' should appear as the chosen APN. If not, you could edit existing APN or tap the +/Add APN button to create a new one with below information:

   a. For Name, enter 'SUNDAY'

   b. For APN, enter 'wbdata' (one word, in small letters, no space)

5. Save the settings and restart your device.

How can I activate my eSIM on my iPhone in Turkey?

For devices set up after June 23, 2020, eSIM activates upon installation. Contact your carrier if it's not enabled.


For devices set up before June 23, 2020, follow the steps below to activate your eSIM. Backup your data, as the process may erase it.


1. Back up your device and update to the latest iOS. Go to Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Erase All Content and Settings.

2. Enter your passcode or Apple ID password to confirm. After erasing, restore your device from the backup and set up your eSIM.


Note: To use roaming data services in Turkey for 91 days or more within a 120-day period, your device's IMEI must be registered with Turkey's Central Equipment Identity Register. Contact CEIR for details. Source: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT211023
