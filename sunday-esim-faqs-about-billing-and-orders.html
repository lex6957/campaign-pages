About Billing & Orders

How can I apply a promotion code or referral code?

When you make a new purhcase, you may enter the code to the field Promotion code on Checkout page and then tap Apply.


Note: All promotional offers are subject to Terms & Conditions. You may click here for more information.

The billing amount is incorrect. What should I do?

On your SUNDAY App:

1. Log into your SUNDAY account

2. Go to Profile section and then tap Contact Us.

3. Choose Billing & Orders for type of enquiry and fill up the remaining parts of the form. Don't forget to provide the details of the issues you encountered, including the Order Number and the disputable amount.


Or you may send an email to enquiry@sunday.com with case details to us. We will reply to you soon.

How can I request for a refund?

On your SUNDAY App:

1. Log into your SUNDAY account

2. Go to Profile section and then tap Contact Us.

3. Choose Billing & Orders for type of enquiry and fill up the remaining parts of the form. Don't forget to provide the details and reason of your refund case.


Or you may send an email to enquiry@sunday.com with details to request for a refund, including the Order Number and the reason for refund.


Note: All requests for refund are subject to our Refund Policy and Terms & Conditions. You may click here for more information.
