About SUNDAY Membership

How can I become a SUNDAY Member?

Download SUNDAY: eSIM Travel App from App Store or Play Store.

Open the App and follow the instruction to register as a SUNDAY Member by providing a verifiable email address and accepting the SUNDAY eSIM General Terms & Conditions and the Privacy Statement.

How do I unsubscribe from marketing emails?

On your Sunday App:

1. Go to Profile section.

2. Select Notification.

3. For the item Marketing email, tap to turn off the toggle button.


Don't want to miss any of our promotions? You may turn this on again anytime.

How do I delete my account?

On your Sunday App:

1. Go to Profile section.

2. Go into Account, tap Delete Account.

3. Read the reminders and tap Yes to confirm your action.


Note: This action will permanently erase your account info and cannot be undone. All purchased eSIM(s) will be terminated and any remaining data packages cannot be used anymore. All the S.miles will also be forfeited.

What is SUNDAY Reward Program?

The SUNDAY Reward Program is our way of showing appreciation to each and every one of you. When you have purchased any SUNDAY Pass through SUNDAY App, you will be given some reward points. The accumulated reward points - we call them S.miles here - can be used as discounts in your future purchases.


You can earn 10 S.miles for every US$1 spent on SUNDAY Pass via SUNDAY App. Next time, when you buy SUNDAY Pass, you may enter the amount of S.miles you want to apply at checkout and the total order value will be discounted accordingly. You are entitled to enjoy a discount of US$1 for every 200 S.miles applied in a transaction.


Note: All Reward Program and S.miles related offers are subject to Terms & Conditions. You may click here for more information.

What is S.mile?

A "S.mile" refers to a point earned in SUNDAY Reward Program. For every US dollar you spend on SUNDAY Passes, you will earn 10 S.miles (unless otherwise specified). Next time, when you buy SUNDAY Pass, you may enter the amount of S.miles you want to apply at checkout and the total order value will be discounted accordingly. You are entitled to enjoy a discount of US$1 for every 200 S.miles applied in a transaction.


You can also earn S.miles by introducing our service to your friends. You will get 200 S.miles for every referred friend who completes their first purchase on SUNDAY App.


Earned S.miles are valid for 365 days from the date they are credited. If you make another purchase within such validity period, any remaining S.miles will automatically extend for another 365 days.
