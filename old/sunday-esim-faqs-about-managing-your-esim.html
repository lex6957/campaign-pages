About Managing your eSIM

How many eSIMs can I install?

The number of eSIMs you can install varies among different devices. Most smartphones can store 2 to 8 eSIM profiles, but you can only turn on ONE eSIM for data at a time. Please check your device's specifications or settings for the exact limit.

Where can I find my eSIM’s ICCID number?

On your SUNDAY App:

1. Go to My eSIM section.

2. Under the corresponding tab of the eSIM, tap Install/View eSIM and you can find the ICCID number.

How do I know which eSIM is being used?

Under My eSIM section, you can see different plans with various status labels.

[In Use] - The data plan is currently in use.

[Ready to Use] - The data plan that is ready to be activated when you arrive the corresponding destination and/or previous data plan is used up.

[Used] - The data plan has already been activated, and its validity period has ended.

[Expired] - The data plan that has not been activated within 60 days after purchase.

How can I add more data to my eSIM?

You can always make new purchase with SUNDAY App.   After selecting your new plan and payment method at the Checkout page, you can choose to add your new plan to any existing eSIM.  Pick the one you want and tap Continue to checkout.  When the order completes, the plan will be added to your selected eSIM.  If you have multiple plans for the same destination in your selected eSIM, the first purchased plan will be activated first.

Or you may go to My eSIM > Reorder Now to purchase data plan for the same destination.

How many eSIM can a SUNDAY Member account support?

Each SUNDAY Member account can support up to ten (10) SUNDAY eSIMs.

However, the number of eSIMs you can install varies among different devices. Most smartphones can store 2 to 8 eSIM profiles, but you can only turn on ONE eSIM for data at a time.

Please check your device's specifications or settings for the exact limit.


For easy management of eSIM, we strongly recommend adding new data plan to your existing SUNDAY eSIM.

Will I be notified when I’m running out of data?

Yes, you will be receiving alerts (SMS + App push notification) when your current data plan only has 100MB left.

You can also keep track of your usage under My eSIM - a dashboard will be showing the total data you have and the amount you have used.

What happens when I run out of data on my eSIM?

You will be receiving alerts (SMS + App push notification) when your current data plan only has 100MB left.

If you have already purchased other data plans on the same eSIM for the same location, the next plan will start automatically and your connection will not be suspended.


If you do not have any other purchased data plans, you may simply buy any add-on plans before you run out of data.

Can I install the same eSIM on multiple devices?

No, you cannot install the same eSIM on multiple devices at the same time.

However, you could install the eSIM on another compatible device (or reinstall it on the same one) after removing the eSIM profile from your current device successfully. Steps to delete an eSIM:

For iOS users

1. Open Settings on your device.

2. Go to Cellular or Mobile Service.

3. Select the eSIM you want to remove and tap Delete eSIM or Remove Cellular/Mobile Plan.


For Android users

1. Open Settings on your device.

2. Go to Connections/Network & Internet, and then choose SIM Manager, Mobile Network or SIM Cards.

3. Select the eSIM you want to remove and tap Delete/Remove.


After removing the eSIM, you may now follow the normal installation steps to install it to another device.

How can I rename my SUNDAY eSIM for easier reference?

On your SUNDAY App:

1. Go to My eSIM section.

2. Under the corresponding tab of the eSIM, tap Install/View eSIM.

3. Inside Install/View eSIM, next to the text field of eSIM Name, you will see a pencil icon.

4. Tap and change the name of your eSIM.


Note: This will only change the eSIM name on SUNDAY App. To change the eSIM label on your device, please go to related device setting.

I lost my phone. Can I transfer my eSIM to a new phone?

To transfer an eSIM to a new phone, you will need to remove it from your original device first. If you have lost your original phone, it is NOT feasible to transfer the eSIM or data plan to your new device.

To continue to use SUNDAY service, please purchase new data plans with a new SUNDAY eSIM by selecting New eSIM on checkout page.
