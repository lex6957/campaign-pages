Getting Started with SUNDAY

How can I buy an eSIM? Do I need to buy eSIM and data plan separately?

You only need to buy the data plan you desire, and it will come with an eSIM if this is your first purchase. Simply follow the steps below.


On your SUNDAY App:

1. Sign up or log into your SUNDAY account.

2. Enter or select your destination on the homepage.

3. Choose the SUNDAY Pass (data plan) you preferred and follow the steps to checkout.

      For newcomer, a new eSIM will be assigned to the data plan when you buy your first data package.

      If you're a repeat customer, you can choose to add the new data package to an existing OR a new eSIM on checkout page.>

4. You will receive an order confirmation email upon successful purchase. Follow the instructions for installation, or go to My eSIM for details.


Note: Before buying any of the SUNDAY Passes (data plans), remember to check that your device is eSIM-compatible and carrier unlocked.

When should I install and/or activate my eSIM?

eSIM Installation

After purchase, you may follow the steps to complete the installation on your eSIM-compatible device under stable Wi-Fi (recommended) network. You are recommended to do this anytime BEFORE you set off.


See Also

How to install eSIM (to the tutorial)


Data Plan Activitation

After successful eSIM installation, you could activate your data plan when you arrive at the destination:

1. Go to the eSIM setting on your device

2. Turn on your SUNDAY eSIM

3. Enable Data Romaing

Your eSIM will then activate ONCE it connects to a supported network.

How to add a new data plan to existing eSIM?

After choosing your payment method at the Checkout page, SUNDAY will ask if you want to add your new plan to any existing eSIM. Choose the one you want and tap Continue to checkout.  Then, the new plan will be purchased and added to the selected eSIM, and you can skip the installation step. Easy!

Note: If you have multiple plans for the same destination in your eSIM, the first purchased plan will be activated first.

Do I need to buy a new eSIM every time for a new data plan?

SUNDAY offers you the flexibility to suit your needs. It is not necessary to get a new eSIM when purchasing a new data plan.

For newcomer, a new eSIM will be assigned to your account when you buy your first data package. Next time when you make a purchase on SUNDAY App, you can choose to add the new data plan to an existing OR a new eSIM on Checkout page.
