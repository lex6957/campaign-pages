When should I activate my eSIM?

After successful eSIM installation, you can follow the steps below to do the setting when you arrive at the destination:

1. Go to the eSIM setting on your device

2. Turn on your SUNDAY eSIM

3. Enable Data Romaing

Your eSIM will then activate ONCE it connects to a supported network.

After I activated the data plan, can I pause it temporarily and turn it on later?

No, you cannnot manually pause any activated plan(s). Once the data plan is activated, its validity period will start counting until the specified expiration date and time.

When will my SUNDAY Pass expire?

Unused SUNDAY Passes will expire 60 days after the date of purchase.  You could check the "Active before" date under My eSIM and make sure you activate and use the Pass before this date.

Activated SUNDAY Passes will expire after their validity period ends or when all data allowance is consumed, whichever comes first. You can always check Start and End Date of the plan(s) in use under My eSIM.

How can I track my current data usage?

On your SUNDAY App:

1. Go to My eSIM section.

2. Tap to select the eSIM that you want to check the usage

3. The dasboard will be showing the following info:

     a. Total GB of the plan

     b. Used GB

     c. Remaining GB

You could also check the Start and End Date of the plan(s) in use. Please note that there could be up to 20 mins delay of information update.

What happens to any unused data?

If you leave a destination with unused data and return to the same location within the validity period, you can still utilize that data on a supported network. However, once the validity period of your data plan expires, any remaining unused data will be forfeited.

You can always check the validity period of your SUNDAY Pass(es) under My eSIM.

Can I use 5G with my eSIM?

SUNDAY eSIMs are compatible with 5G mobile devices/networks, but the speed may vary depending on your device and location.  You can find out if the SUNDAY Pass you plan to purchase supports a 5G network under section Additional Information > Coverage & Operator.

Can I make phone calls or send SMS with my eSIM?

Currently SUNDAY only provides data service. You can use internet-based calls and messages (e.g. Whatsapp) but not traditional voice calls or SMS.

Can I use my eSIM as a tether or hotspot for another device?

Yes, you can. Just note that speed of the hotspot may vary based on your network connection and the number of devices connected.
